Foster Moore

Architecture and Development

Since 2003 Holloware have provided development resources for Foster Moore to assist with the enterprise Java development of the Ministry of Business, Innovation and Employment (MBIE) registries.

The registries developed for the MBIE include:

  • New Zealand Companies Office
  • Motor Vehicle Traders Register
  • Insolvency and Trustee Service
  • Radio Spectrum Management
  • Crown Minerals

Our involvement in these sites includes everything from architecture design to final implementation.
